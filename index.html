This site was started by two inquisitive, albeit miseducated, autodidacts on a quest to unravel the infinitely complex modern world. Fueled by a genuine curiosity for knowledge, and a fearless commitment to free speech, we invite listeners and readers to navigate the labyrinthine tapestry of history, culture, and science as we seek to deepen our understanding and broaden our tainted perspectives. We do not come from academia. We do not pretend to be experts. Armed with nothing more than an open mind and the moral tenet that all humans ought to be treated equally and with dignity, we roam the vast information landscape to advance dialog and promote peace.
